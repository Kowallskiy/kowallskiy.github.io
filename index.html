Quantum Limitless

Quantum Limitless is an algorithmic trading application that lets you build, backtest, and deploy your AI-powered trading strategies seamlessly. No coding is required. Everything is executed automatically.

My GitHub
▶

/project_overview

The application is designed to simplify the process of creating automated trading strategies. You can easily:

  • Select an ML/AI model based on your requirements.
  • Build a unique portfolio of stocks or cryptocurrencies.
  • Design custom features and signals from the OHCLV data.
  • Train an ML/AI model on your custom dataset with your custom hyperparameters, which always guarantees unique results.
  • Backtest your strategy on historical data.
  • Deploy a live trading AI model to real or paper trading accounts (Alpaca).

/key_features

The application has an intuitive interface for non-coders. Whether you are playing with different strategies or testing a long-term portfolio approach, the app covers it all.

/technical_stack

Built using Python, PyTorch, Scikit-Learn, Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Yfinance, Gradio, Zipline, Pyfolio, Pyro (probabilistic programming), Docker, Git, AWS.
